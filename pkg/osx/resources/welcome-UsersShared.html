Welcome to the RTcmix Installer. You will be guided through the steps necessary to install this software.

After installation, you may see an error message about a failure to write the receipt. Don’t worry about this; just keep going.

WARNING: This installation will move your current “.profile” to “.profile.before_rtcmix_install”. If you want to save your current profile, it’s better to do it manually before installing RTcmix.
